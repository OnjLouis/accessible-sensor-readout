Asus ROG / TUF Support Plug-In
================================

This is an experimental, opt-in Sensor Readout hardware plug-in for Asus
ROG/TUF-style laptops. It reads Asus ATKACPI temperature and fan duty-cycle
values where available, and attempts fan control on models that accept the
known ATKACPI fan curve or fan range calls.

Initial plug-in work by Jason Fayre and Claude Code.
Integration, diagnostics, accessibility, and packaging by Sensor Readout
contributors.
