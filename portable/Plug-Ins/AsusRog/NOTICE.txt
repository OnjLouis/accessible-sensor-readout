Asus ROG Support Plug-In
========================

This is an experimental, opt-in Sensor Readout hardware plug-in for Asus
ROG laptops and desktops. It reads Asus ATKACPI or ASUS WMI temperature and
fan tachometer values where available. Fan control remains limited to laptop
models that accept the known ATKACPI fan curve or fan range calls; desktop
tower profiles are treated as read-only by this plug-in and should use
LibreHardwareMonitor/PawnIO fan controls when the motherboard exposes them.

Initial plug-in work by Jason Fayre and Claude Code.

contributors.
